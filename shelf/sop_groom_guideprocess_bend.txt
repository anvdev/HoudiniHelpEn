= Bend Guides =

"""Bends curves in a certain direction and by a certain angle. """

This tool lets you bend curves in a certain direction and by a certain angle, in the direction the root is pointing.

Clicking [Icon:FUR/orientation] __Bend Guides__ on the __Guide Process__ tab will put down a [Guide Process SOP|Node:sop/guideprocess] with the __Operation__ parameter set to __Bend__.  For more information, see the [Guide Process SOP|Node:sop/guideprocess] help page.
